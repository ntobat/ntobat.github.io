Google Refuge

A mobile device-based solution for those affected by the Syrian Refugee Crisis.

Introducing Google Refuge.

The Syrian Refugee Crisis is the largest refugee displacement event in human history. As of September 2018, over 5.6 million Syrians have been forced to flee their homes from the violent conflict.

Google Refuge provides connectivity for those who have lost everything else.

Seeking Connection

Displaced refugees spend 1/3 of their income or resources on communication in an attempt to seek asylum, get short term and long term assistance, or locate loved ones.

There is a desparate need for information services to coordinate with separated loved ones, manage documentation, information and resources. At Google, we have the resources to help in this area – so we are.

How It Works

Refugees need more than positive vibes sent their way. They need a beacon of hope.

Google Beacons are wifi-enabling network towers. Each Beacon is stocked with over 1000 Google Light Phones.

The Beacons tether together wirelessly, creating a greater area of connectivity for the phones to operate on.

Users can then access a specialized suite of Google products, all available through a Google Account.

Each Beacon also serves as a solar powered charging station to provide sustainable usage for each phone as needed.

The Google Light Phone

Google Light Phones are extra durable and data-efficient smart phones which connect to the internet via WiFi.
